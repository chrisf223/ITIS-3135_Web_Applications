CCI BUDDIES

Welcome to CCI Buddies!

This platform serves as a tool for UNC Charlotte CCI Students to easily connect with one another in order to easily form study groups. No longer will students have to feel nervous about eaching out to other students for help. They can simply use CCI Buddies!

In addition to this, this platform also provides students with a number of resources related to the UNCC CCI program.

Study groups can be scheduled based on specific classes, or they can also simply be general study groups!

Feel free to navigate throughout the platform and make sure to contact us with any questions or concerns that you may have.
